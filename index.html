What is DevVerDojo?

DevVerDojo is a modern learning dojo for developers who want to grow with the times. Built on the idea of semantic versioning, you'll evolve through Major concepts (like AI, clean code, and critical thinking), Minor lessons (bite-sized skills), and Patches (ongoing updates in tools, workflows, and best practices).

Who is it for?

Whether you're fresh out of a bootcamp, early in your dev career, or just AI-curious, this is your space to grow. Learn to code smarter, think deeper, and collaborate with AI tools effectively.

What You'll Learn

  • Prompt engineering for real-world dev work
  • Problem-solving strategies with AI assistance
  • Project-based learning with versioned progress
  • Ethical and responsible AI usage
  • Collaboration and community-led sprints

My Story

I'm Jim Shelly—a lifelong technologist with over 30 years of experience in software development and 5 years as a bootcamp instructor. I've helped hundreds of people break into tech, grow in confidence, and land their first roles.

This isn’t just a course. It’s a dojo. A place to evolve your mindset, upgrade your developer stack, and become the kind of creative, adaptable problem-solver the world truly needs.
